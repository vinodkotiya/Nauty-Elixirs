The Nauty Story

CRAFTED WITH INTENTION

Nauty Elixirs began its journey on the rugged shores of Salt Spring Island, BC, where founder Sandra discovered the transformative power of herbal alternatives. Inspired by the island's raw beauty and deep connection to the land, she envisioned creating more than just a beverage—she dreamed of crafting a ritual that would nurture both body and spirit.

Each batch is meticulously handcrafted using certified organic herbs and botanicals, thoughtfully sourced from sustainable farms. Sandra's process combines ancient wisdom with modern innovation, carefully selecting ingredients like roasted chicory root, dandelion, and chaga mushrooms not only for their robust flavors but also for their natural wellness benefits.

The brand's motto "Feel Naughty, Stay Grounded" reflects Sandra's belief that indulgence and wellness can coexist harmoniously. Through Nauty Elixirs, she invites others to join her journey toward mindful living, offering a unique blend that celebrates nature's bounty while maintaining an unwavering commitment to quality and authenticity.

Sandra, CEO & Founder
