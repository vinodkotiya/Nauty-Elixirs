Take a look to make your perfect cup

Measure your perfect
portion

Start your ritual by measuring ½ tablespoon of our earthy elixir blend for every 8 ounces of water. This perfect ratio ensures optimal flavor extraction.

Heat your water mindfully

Bring fresh, filtered water to a gentle boil. Let it cool slightly - this ensures the delicate herbs and mushrooms release their full potential without becoming bitter.

Begin the steeping
journey

Pour your hot water over the measured elixir blend. Watch as the rich, dark color begins to develop, releasing earthy aromas that signal the start of your ritual.

Practice patience with
8-minute steep

Allow your elixir to steep for a full 8 minutes. This precise timing allows the roots and mushrooms to fully release their beneficial compounds and rich flavor.

Customize your experience

After steeping, your elixir is ready to enjoy as is, or personalize it with your favorite milk alternative and natural sweetener for a comforting, coffee-like experience.

Mindful moments

Take a moment to breathe deeply, appreciate the aromatic experience, and sip mindfully. Feel yourself becoming more grounded with each nourishing cup.

The Nauty Story

CRAFTED WITH INTENTION

Nauty Elixirs was born on beautiful Salt Spring Island when founder Sandra discovered the power of herbal alternatives during her own wellness journey. Frustrated by coffee alternatives that lacked depth and character, she spent two years perfecting this unique blend.

Each batch is hand-crafted in small quantities to ensure quality and freshness, maintaining the connection between nature's gifts and your daily ritual.
